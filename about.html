About District Comedy

District Comedy strives to be an inclusive comedy club, available virtually. Its inception was a reaction to the pandemic of 2020, and hopes to provide a creative outlet for those no longer able to perform at live venues. However, over time, a strong community has been developed, and it is the intention of District Comedy to continue to provide virtual events even when the pandemic has subsided, as the digital medium provides accessibility to those of varying ability, financial situations, and location. These factors have been essential in engaging with a more inclusive community and elevating diverse voices. Additionally, when it is safe to do so, District Comedy hopes to expand its offerings to live venues, although these principles will remain the core values of the organization. Tom Beheler is the founder and overarching administrator of District Comedy, although it is hosts, performers, and audience members that make up its heart.
